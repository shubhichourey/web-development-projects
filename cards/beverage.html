BEVERAGES

MILK

Milk is a fundamental dairy product that has been a part of human diets for centuries. It's a rich source of essential nutrients, including calcium, protein, and vitamins like D and B12. Beyond its nutritional value, milk plays a central role in countless culinary creations, from creamy desserts to comforting hot beverages.

TEA

Tea is a globally cherished beverage, steeped in history and cultural significance. Derived from the Camellia sinensis plant, it comes in various forms, from the delicate green teas of Asia to the robust black teas of the West. Tea is not just a drink; it's a comforting ritual, a source of antioxidants, and a symbol of hospitality.

COCKTAIL

Cocktails are a diverse and delightful category of mixed drinks that have evolved into an art form. These skillfully crafted concoctions combine various spirits, liqueurs, juices, and syrups to create a wide range of flavors and experiences.

MATCHA

Matcha is a finely ground, vibrant green tea powder with deep roots in Japanese culture. This unique and versatile ingredient is renowned for its rich, earthy flavor and vibrant emerald hue. It is created by carefully shading the tea leaves before harvesting, which enhances its chlorophyll content and distinctive taste.

MOJITO

A Mojito is a refreshing and iconic cocktail that hails from the vibrant streets of Havana, Cuba. Known for its invigorating blend of fresh mint leaves, zesty lime juice, sugar, and white rum, the Mojito is a tropical delight that awakens the senses. Its crisp and citrusy flavors, coupled with the cooling sensation of muddled mint.

LEMONADE

Lemonade is a timeless and thirst-quenching classic in the world of beverages. This simple yet refreshing drink is made from the zesty juice of lemons, combined with a touch of sweetness and water. Its tart and tangy flavor, coupled with the invigorating chill of ice, makes it a perfect companion on hot summer days.

JUICE

Juice, a liquid treasure extracted from fruits and vegetables, is a delightful elixir of nature's flavors. Whether it's the sweet, sun-kissed essence of freshly squeezed orange juice or the earthy depth of a kale and apple blend, juices offer a burst of vitamins, minerals, and natural sugars.

BEER

Beer, one of the world's oldest and most widely consumed alcoholic beverages, is a beverage steeped in tradition and variety. Crafted from malted grains, hops, water, and yeast, beer offers a vast spectrum of flavors and styles, from light lagers to robust stouts. .

SOFT DRINKS

Soft drinks, also known as sodas or carbonated beverages, are effervescent delights in the world of refreshments. Typically flavored with sweeteners, artificial or natural, and carbonated water, they offer a wide range of flavors, from classic cola to citrusy spritzers.
